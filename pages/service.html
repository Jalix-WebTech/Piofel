Our Services

Maize farming

We engage in efficient maize cultivation using modern farming practices to ensure high yield, quality harvests, and sustainable food production.

Cassava farming

Our cassava farming focuses on healthy crop management and improved varieties to deliver reliable, high-quality produce for both consumption and processing.

Drainage and Irrigation

We design and construct durable drainage and irrigation systems that improve water management, prevent flooding, and support consistent agricultural productivity.

Piggery and poultry

We operate well-managed piggery and poultry farms, prioritizing animal health, proper feeding, and hygienic practices to supply quality livestock products.

Electric and gas engineering

We provide professional electrical and gas engineering services, delivering safe, efficient, and compliant solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial needs.

Feed Production

We produce high-quality livestock feed formulated to promote healthy growth, improve productivity, and meet nutritional standards.
